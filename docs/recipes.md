## Breakfasts

### Overnight Oats (Base + Variations)
**No knives:** measure & stir.  
**Serves:** 1 per jar • **Keeps:** 3 days chilled

**Base (per jar)**
- 50 g rolled oats  
- 120 ml milk (or half milk/half yoghurt)  
- 2 tsp chia or 1 tsp ground flax  
- Pinch salt

**Stir-ins (choose 1–2)**
- 1 tsp honey or maple, 1 tbsp peanut butter, handful frozen berries, 1 tbsp choc chips, ½ mashed banana

**Method**
1. Add all base ingredients to a jar/tub. Stir in chosen stir-ins.  
2. Chill overnight. Top with seeds/fruit in the morning.

**Variations**
- **PB & Banana:** peanut butter + mashed banana.  
- **Chocolate:** 1 tsp cocoa + choc chips.  
- **Berry:** frozen berries + yoghurt.

### Banana Pancakes
**No knives:** mash with fork.  
**Makes:** 8–10 small pancakes

**Ingredients**
- 1 ripe banana (mashed)  
- 1 egg (beaten)  
- 4–6 tbsp plain flour (to a thick batter)  
- Splash milk, pinch salt, little oil/butter for frying

**Method**
1. Stir everything to a thick batter.  
2. Fry tablespoons of batter 1–2 min each side.  
3. Serve with fruit/honey.

### Homemade Bagels (Cold-Rise)
**Freezeable** • **No knives:** shape by hand, snip toppings if needed.

**Ingredients**
- 500 g strong bread flour  
- 7 g fast-action yeast  
- 2 tsp salt  
- ~300 ml warm water  
- 2 tbsp sugar (plus 1 tbsp for boiling water)

**Method**
1. Mix flour, yeast, salt, water to a dough. Knead 8–10 min.  
2. Cover & **cold-rise overnight** in the fridge.  
3. Divide into 8, shape balls, poke hole with finger, stretch gently.  
4. Boil in water with 1 tbsp sugar: 1 min each side.  
5. Bake 200 °C 18–20 min. Cool; **freeze** extras; toast from frozen.

---

### Wholemeal Rolls
**Ingredients**
- 500 g strong wholemeal flour  
- 7 g yeast • 2 tsp salt • 1 tbsp oil • ~320 ml warm water

**Method**
1. Mix → knead 8–10 min → rise 1 h.  
2. Divide into 10–12; shape rolls; prove 30–40 min.  
3. Bake 200 °C 15–18 min. **Freeze** cooled rolls.

---

### Chapatis / Flatbreads
**Ingredients**
- 250 g wholemeal flour • pinch salt • ~170 ml warm water

**Method**
1. Mix to soft dough; rest 15 min.  
2. Divide into 6–8; pat/roll thin.  
3. Dry-fry in hot pan 30–60 sec each side until blistered.  
4. **Freeze** between sheets.

---

### Fridge Oat Slice (No-Bake)
**No knives:** press & chill.  
**Makes:** 12 bars • **Keeps:** 5 days chilled

**Ingredients**
- 250 g rolled oats  
- 2 ripe bananas (well mashed)  
- 60 g honey or syrup  
- 60 g peanut butter (or butter)  
- 60 g sultanas or choc chips  
- Pinch salt

**Method**
1. Stir all ingredients.  
2. Press into lined 20×20 cm tin.  
3. Chill 2–3 h; slice with dough scraper. **Freeze** squares if you like.

## Lunches

### Chicken & Veg Soup with Dumplings
**Uses roast chicken & stock** • **Reheat/Freeze soup (no dumplings)**

**Ingredients**
- 1–1.2 L chicken stock (homemade or cubes)  
- 2 carrots & 1 parsnip (peeled, chunked with scraper)  
- 2 potatoes (diced)  
- 1 stick celery (snipped) or ½ tsp celery salt  
- 1 tin beans/lentils, rinsed  
- Handful frozen peas  
- 200–300 g cooked chicken, shredded with forks  
- Herbs: bay/thyme/parsley, salt & pepper

**Dumplings**
- 200 g self-raising flour • 100 g Atora (veg or beef) • pinch salt • cold water

**Method**
1. Stock + veg + herbs → simmer 20–25 min until soft.  
2. Add beans, peas, chicken; 10 min more.  
3. Dumplings: mix flour, suet, salt; add water to soft dough; roll 8 balls.  
4. Drop in; lid on; simmer 20 min.  
**Storage:** Cool; **freeze** soup without dumplings. Add fresh dumplings when reheating.

### Microwave Veggie Bowl (Single-Serve)
**Knife-free, 6 mins**

**Ingredients (per bowl)**
- 1 cup frozen peas & sweetcorn  
- ½ cup butter beans/chickpeas (rinsed)  
- ½ stock cube + splash water  
- Pinch mixed herbs, pepper  
- 1 tbsp grated cheese **or** 1 tsp seeds

**Method**
1. Everything in a microwave jug; cover.  
2. Microwave 5–6 min; stir; top with cheese or seeds.  
3. Roll/wrap on the side.

### Butterbean, Tomato & Feta Pan Salad
**Makes 2–3 lunches — good warm or cold**

**Ingredients**
- 1 tbsp olive oil • 2 cloves garlic (crushed/paste)  
- 250 g cherry tomatoes  
- 2 tins butterbeans, rinsed  
- 1 lemon (zest & juice)  
- 1 tsp dried oregano • ½ tsp chilli flakes • 1 tsp honey  
- 150 g feta, crumbled

**Method**
1. Oil + garlic in pan; 30 sec.  
2. Add tomatoes; cook till skins split.  
3. Add beans, lemon, oregano, chilli, honey; warm through.  
4. Stir in feta till creamy. Season.  
**Keeps:** 2–3 days chilled.

---

### Pea & Veg Salads (Mix & Match)
**Base:** defrosted peas + lemon juice/zest + olive oil + salt/pepper.  
**Add:** one veg + one cheese + one herb + optional nuts/seeds.

- **Veg:** courgette ribbons (peeler) / grated carrot / shredded cabbage (grater) / mixed peppers (frozen, defrosted) / edamame  
- **Cheese:** feta / mozzarella (tear) / grated cheddar  
- **Herbs:** oregano + honey (Greek-style) / mint + chilli / parsley + lemon / dill + yoghurt  
- **Crunch:** sunflower/pumpkin/sesame seeds, chopped nuts

## Dinners

### Air Fryer Chicken, Potatoes & Veg Traybake
**All cooks together**

**Ingredients**
- 600–800 g chicken thighs/breast, snipped to chunks  
- 600 g baby potatoes, skin on  
- 3 carrots or 2 parsnips, peeled if you like  
- 300 g mixed peppers/broccoli (fresh or frozen)  
- 2 tbsp oil • 1 tsp garlic/onion granules • 1 tsp rosemary/thyme • salt & pepper

**Method**
1. Toss everything in oil & seasonings.  
2. Air fry 190 °C for 25–30 min, shaking halfway.  
3. Optional quick gravy: veg water + granules + pinch dried sage.  
**Leftovers:** sandwiches, fried rice, soup.

---

### Chicken Stir-Fry with Noodles
**Ingredients**
- 300 g cooked chicken, shredded  
- 350 g frozen stir-fry veg mix  
- 2 cloves garlic (crushed) • 1 tsp ginger paste  
- 300 g noodles (cooked/drained)  
- 3 tbsp soy • 1 tbsp rice vinegar • 1 tsp Chinese 5-spice  
- Snipped spring onions, toasted nuts to serve

**Method**
1. Hot pan: oil + garlic + ginger; add veg (5 min).  
2. Add chicken + soy + vinegar + 5-spice.  
3. Toss in noodles; heat through.  
4. Top with spring onion & nuts.

### Chicken Fried Rice
**Best with day-old rice**

**Ingredients**
- 250–300 g cooked rice  
- 250 g mixed frozen veg  
- 250 g cooked chicken, shredded  
- 2–3 tbsp soy • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)  
- 2 eggs (optional for others)

**Method**
1. Oil + garlic → veg → chicken.  
2. Add rice; fry till hot.  
3. (Optional) Scramble eggs on one side; mix in.  
4. Soy + sesame oil; serve.

### Garlic Mushroom Spaghetti
**Ingredients**
- 300–350 g spaghetti  
- 400 g mushrooms, brushed & torn  
- 3 cloves garlic (crushed)  
- 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil  
- 1 tsp dried thyme  
- Splash milk/cream (optional)  
- Parmesan/cheddar to finish (optional)

**Method**
1. Cook spaghetti; save ½ cup pasta water.  
2. Pan: butter + oil → garlic 30 sec → mushrooms + thyme till golden.  
3. Add splash milk/cream if using; season.  
4. Toss with spaghetti + pasta water to gloss. Cheese if you like.

### Lentil Dhal with Chapatis
**Freezes well**

**Ingredients**
- 300 g red lentils (rinsed)  
- 1 small onion (grated or halved & removed later)  
- 3 cloves garlic (crushed) • 1 tsp ginger paste  
- 1 tsp turmeric • 1 tsp cumin • 1 tsp garam masala • chilli to taste  
- 1 tin chopped tomatoes  
- 800–900 ml veg/chicken stock  
- 1–2 tbsp peanut butter (optional richness)  
- Lemon, coriander (optional)

**Method**
1. Pot: lentils, onion, garlic, ginger, spices, tomatoes, stock.  
2. Simmer 30–35 min, stirring.  
3. Stir in peanut butter; lemon to taste.  
4. Serve with **[Chapatis](#chapatis--flatbreads)**.  
**Freeze:** flat in bags.

### Butterbean Stew with Dumplings
**Comforting; reheats well**

**Ingredients**
- 1 onion (grated) • 1 pepper (snipped)  
- 2 carrots, 1 parsnip, 2 potatoes (scraper-chopped)  
- 2 tins butterbeans (rinsed)  
- 1 tin tomatoes + 1 tbsp tomato purée  
- 1 tsp paprika • 1 tsp thyme • salt & pepper  
- 800 ml veg stock

**Dumplings:** as per soup (200 g SR flour + 100 g suet + water)

**Method**
1. Pan: soften onion/pepper in a little oil.  
2. Add root veg, beans, tomatoes, purée, paprika, thyme, stock.  
3. Simmer 25–30 min; season.  
4. Drop dumplings; lid on 20 min.

### Tomato & Herb Pasta
**10–15 minute sauce**

**Ingredients**
- 1 tbsp oil • 2 cloves garlic (crushed)  
- 1 tbsp tomato purée  
- 1 tin whole plum tomatoes (snip in tin)  
- 150 ml stock  
- 1 tsp dried oregano/basil • pinch sugar • salt/pepper  
- 350 g pasta • cheese to serve

**Method**
1. Oil + garlic 30 sec; stir in purée 1 min.  
2. Add tomatoes (snipped) + stock + herbs + sugar; simmer 10–15 min.  
3. Toss with pasta; cheese on top.

### Pizza Night (Dough + Simple Sauce)
**Dough (2 large or 4 small)**
- 500 g strong flour • 7 g yeast • 10 g salt  
- 320–340 ml warm water • 1 tbsp oil

Mix, knead 8–10 min, rise 1 h. Shape, top, bake as hot as your oven goes (230–250 °C).  
**Sauce:** 1 tin tomatoes (snipped) + 1 tbsp purée + pinch sugar + oregano + salt/pepper (no cook).  
**Toppings:** mozzarella, peppers, onions (grated/sliced with mandolin guard), olives, leftover chicken/veg.  
**Freeze:** double dough; freeze half for next Friday.

## Soups & Sides

### Roast Parsnip Soup
**Ingredients**
- 800 g parsnips, peeled (safety peeler)  
- 2 tbsp oil + 1 tsp cumin + salt/pepper  
- 1 L veg/chicken stock • squeeze lemon • cream/milk (optional)

**Method**
1. Toss parsnips with oil, cumin, salt; roast/air-fry till caramelised.  
2. Simmer with stock 10 min; blend smooth; lemon & seasoning to taste; swirl cream if using.  
**Freeze:** yes.

---

### Simple Vegetable Soup
**Method**
1. Pot: 1 grated onion, 2 grated carrots, 2 snipped celery sticks; soften in a little oil.  
2. Add 2 potatoes (diced), 1 tin beans, stock to cover, herbs; simmer till tender.  
3. Blend half if you like; season.  
**Freeze:** yes.

## Sweet Treats

### Double Chocolate Cookies (Freeze-Ahead Balls)
**Ingredients**
- 200 g dark chocolate + 60 g butter (melt together)  
- 1 tin condensed milk  
- 300 g self-raising flour + pinch salt  
- 200–300 g chocolate chips

**Method**
1. Mix melted choc/butter with condensed milk.  
2. Stir into flour/salt; fold in chips.  
3. Chill 15–20 min; scoop balls (35+).  
4. Bake 170 °C fan 10–12 min (9–12 per tray).  
**Freeze:** raw balls; bake from frozen +1–2 min.

**Variations**
- **Biscoff:** swirl 2–3 tbsp Biscoff through dough; top baked cookies with crushed biscuits.  
- **Fruit & Nut:** swap some chips for chopped nuts/raisins.  
- **“Vanilla chip”**: omit cocoa (use white/milk chips) & add citrus zest.

### Harry & Mum’s Chocolate Traybake
**Sponge**
- Simple Stork sponge (250 g Stork, 250 g sugar, 4 eggs, 220 g SR flour + 30 g cocoa, pinch salt, splash milk if needed)

**Middle “blobs” (optional)**
- Small chilled balls of Nutella or cocoa-butter mixture dotted over batter

**Ganache**
- 1 tin condensed milk + 150 g dark chocolate + 40 g butter (melt gently, cool to spread)

**Method**
1. Bake sponge in lined 20×30 cm tin, 170 °C fan ~22–25 min.  
2. Cool; top with ganache; grate chocolate over.  
3. Slice 20–24 squares. **Freeze** well.

### Lemon Drizzle Traybake (+ Citrus Variations)
**Sponge**
- 250 g SR flour • 200 g sugar • 200 g Stork/butter  
- 4 eggs • zest of 2 lemons • pinch salt

**Drizzle**
- Juice of 2 lemons + 120 g granulated sugar (stir)

**Method**
1. Beat butter/sugar; add eggs; fold in flour + zest + salt.  
2. Bake 20×30 cm tin, 170 °C fan ~22–25 min.  
3. Prick hot cake; pour over drizzle; cool.  
**Variations:** use orange or lime; or make a **citrus ganache** (condensed milk + butter + zest/juice, warmed then cooled).

### No-Bake Lime Cheesecake Pots
**Ingredients**
- 150 g biscuits (bag + rolling pin)  
- 60 g butter (melt)  
- 1 tin condensed milk  
- 2 tubs cream cheese (approx. 450–500 g)  
- Zest & juice of 3–4 limes

**Method**
1. Mix crumbs + butter; press into ramekins.  
2. Beat condensed milk + cream cheese + zest; stir in lime juice (it thickens).  
3. Spoon over; chill 3 h; fruit on top.

### Malt Loaf Traybake (Sticky Squares)
**Ingredients**
- 200 g sultanas  
- 150 ml hot tea  
- 150 g malt extract  
- 75 g dark brown sugar  
- 200 g self-raising flour  
- 1 tsp baking powder • pinch salt  
- 2 eggs

**Method**
1. Soak sultanas in tea 20 min.  
2. Stir in malt & sugar; beat in eggs.  
3. Fold in flour, baking powder, salt.  
4. Bake lined 20×30 cm tray, 150 °C fan, 35–40 min.  
5. Cool; wrap; slice next day for proper stickiness. **Freeze** squares.

### Crystallised Ginger Loaf
**Ingredients**
- 200 g self-raising flour • pinch salt  
- 100 g butter/Stork • 100 g light brown sugar  
- 2 tbsp golden syrup  
- 1 tsp ground ginger • ½ tsp cinnamon  
- 2 eggs • 3 tbsp milk  
- 75 g crystallised ginger (snipped or blitzed)  
- (Optional) 50 g sultanas

**Method**
1. Beat butter & sugar; add eggs, syrup, milk.  
2. Fold in flour, spices, ginger (and sultanas).  
3. Bake 2 lb loaf tin, 170 °C fan, 40–45 min.  
4. Cool fully before slicing; keeps well wrapped.

### Pavlova (Make-Ahead Meringue)
**Ingredients**
- 4 egg whites • 220 g caster sugar  
- 1 tsp cornflour • 1 tsp white vinegar  
- 300 ml double cream (whipped)  
- Berries to top

**Method**
1. Heat 120 °C fan. Whisk whites to soft peaks; gradually add sugar to stiff glossy peaks.  
2. Fold in cornflour & vinegar.  
3. Spread onto lined tray (draw a circle on paper first); build sides for a well.  
4. Bake 1 h; **turn oven off**; leave to cool inside (best overnight).  
5. Top with whipped cream & berries before serving.
